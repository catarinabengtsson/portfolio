The idea of ​​the app is to simplify the purchase process by applying a shopping cart concept.

Only when an item has been reserved and a transaction has started does the buyer's adress become visible to the seller. Once the item has arrived and is approved by the buyer, the transaction is complete. With gideon, the idea is to eliminate misleading and unclear images by guiding the seller in the creation of the ad.
