About me

Download CV

I am Catarina Bengtsson, a multidisciplinary creative from Gothenburg. I would describe myself as a passionate designer who strives to understand the user and develop innovative concepts and ideas through design theory.

I recently graduated from Malmö University with a bachelor's degree and a major in product design. My skills lie in UX/UI Design, web design, and graphic design. The reason I chose to study design is that I have always admired and wanted to understand the interaction between people and products, as well as the visual message conveyed through colors and shapes. I've always found it fascinating to observe people's subconscious behaviors when they engage with products. Understanding what influences a person's actions when they interact with a product intrigues me.

During my studies, I collaborated on multiple design projects. Through these projects, I have gained a deep understanding of how to lead design projects using design theory, including processes such as user research, workshops, and prototypes. My ambitions for my future work are to contribute with what I have already learned, but above all, to continue developing my skills and to embrace new tasks and challenges that drive me forward.
