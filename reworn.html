The app aims to simplify the purchasing process by implementing a shopping cart feature.

The buyer's address becomes visible to the seller only after an item has been reserved and a transaction initiated. Upon the item's arrival and approval by the buyer, the transaction concludes. Reworn aims to eliminate misleading and unclear images by assisting sellers in crafting their ads.

The concept behind the visual identity was to establish a modern, youthful, and confident aesthetic characterized by bold contrasts. A consistent yellow theme was chosen to evoke energy and playfulness throughout.


Upon the creation of the logo, the intention was to embody simplicity and attitude. The selected font is robust and weighty, while the circular shape exudes playfulness. The name Reworn is a paronomasia on reborn.
