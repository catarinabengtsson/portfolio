What I do

Proficient in UX/UI design, web design and graphic design. I have a passion for innovative and intuitive design and a solid interest in creating communication concepts and visual identities.

Web & UX/UI-design

I believe in less is more, a clear and simple design that is easy to understand but still has that little extra to improve the user's experience.

Branding & identity

For me, branding and identity are about recognition and symbolization. What the brand symbolizes and how it can be reflected in the visual identity through recognition.

Graphic design & illustration

This is the icing on the cake. Communicating a visual message through color and shape, preferably with a touch of humor, is undoubtedly one of the best things I know.
